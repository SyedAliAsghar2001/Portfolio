Zeta Science Forum

2024 - Present Co-Founder & CEO

I co-founded Zeta Science Forum along my friends, a student-led initiative dedicated to advancing quantum computing, physics, and science outreach. Through hands-on workshops, competitions, and online resources, we aim to spark curiosity and provide students with opportunities to engage with cutting-edge science.

Our mission is to make advanced scientific knowledge accessible to students in Pakistan, bridging the gap between global research trends and local education. We collaborate with researchers, organize interactive events, and create open learning materials to build a community where young scientists can learn, explore, and grow together.

ENIRP Mentor

7th Sep - 30 Oct 2025 EmpowerED Pakistan Initiative

I am serving as a mentor in the EmpowerED National Interdisciplinary Research Program (ENIRP), an 8-week online initiative (Sept 7 – Oct 30) designed to equip students with the fundamentals of academic research.

Through interactive sessions on YouTube, Zoom, and Google Classroom, participants are trained to explore interdisciplinary topics, develop research proposals, and gain hands-on experience in conducting and presenting their work.

As a mentor, my role is to guide students in formulating research questions, building methodology, and refining their projects to align with academic standards—helping them take their first step into the world of scholarly research.
